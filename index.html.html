Artificial intelligence (AI), is intelligence demonstrated by machines, unlike the naturalintelligence displayed by humansand animals. Leading AI textbooks define the field as the
study of "intelligent agents": any device that perceives its environment and takes actions that maximize its chance of successfully achieving its goals. Colloquially, the term
"artificial intelligence" is often used to describe machines (or computers) that mimic "cognitive" functions that humans associate with the human mind, such as "learning" and "problem solving".
